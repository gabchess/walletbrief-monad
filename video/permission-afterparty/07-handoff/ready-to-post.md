The party ends. Your token approvals might not.

WalletBrief scans any Monad address, shows which approvals are still active, and simulates a revoke before you sign.

Built for Spark on Monad.

https://walletbrief-production.up.railway.app/

## Creative process and music note

We built the film around one prop: a club wristband that still worked the next morning. Actor-led scenes tell the story. Real WalletBrief and MonadVision screens prove it. The music is an original 96 BPM pulse, mixed with room sound and Gabe's narration.

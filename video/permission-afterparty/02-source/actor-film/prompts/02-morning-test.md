## Direction

Use the start frame as the exact opening composition. Keep Nina's face, cream T-shirt, right-wrist band, apartment, laptop, mug, and table unchanged. Add no jewelry, logos, readable text, extra wristbands, warped hands, or duplicate objects. Keep the laptop screen dark and blank for later replacement.

0–4 seconds: One fixed intimate medium shot with a slow micro-push. Nina tries once to peel the band with her left thumb and index finger. The band flexes against the skin but stays closed. Her right forearm keeps its weight on the table. She frowns at the band.

4–7 seconds: In the same uncut shot, Nina releases the band and moves her right hand once to the trackpad. The laptop stays blank and dark. The band keeps one broad chartreuse glow on the same wrist.

7–10 seconds: The camera holds. Nina makes one short trackpad motion, leans toward the laptop, and reads the blank tracked-screen position. Her expression shifts from confusion to focused concern. No cut, new prop, or second camera move.

Native sound: quiet room, cloth and band contact, chair creak, one trackpad click. No music or dialogue.

## Direction

Use the start frame as the exact opening composition. Keep Nina's face, cream T-shirt, right-wrist band, apartment, laptop, mug, and table unchanged. Add no jewelry, logos, readable text, extra wristbands, warped hands, or duplicate objects. Keep the laptop screen dark and blank for later replacement.

0–3 seconds: Fixed medium close-up. Nina makes one deliberate trackpad click with her right index finger. Only after the click, the band glow fades smoothly to dark over one second. She watches it, then exhales. Use one subtle rack focus from her face to the band.

3–6 seconds: Hard cut to a fixed tabletop close-up. The dark band opens at one simple seam, slips off her right wrist under gravity, and lands beside the laptop and mug with real weight. Her hand stays natural and still after release.

6–8 seconds: Hard cut back to a medium shot. Nina lifts the mug once and gives a small relieved smile. The dark band stays on the table. Leave clean negative space at frame right.

Native sound: trackpad click, soft electronic power-down, quiet exhale, light plastic drop, room tone. No music or dialogue.

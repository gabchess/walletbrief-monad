# Permission Afterparty visual system

## Style prompt

A lived-in crypto short film. Wet blue street light and warm club light give way to a quiet cream morning. WalletBrief enters as one clear proof panel, not a deck. The chartreuse wristband ties the story to the product.

## Colors

- `#0d0d0c`: canvas and end card
- `#f4f2ec`: main type
- `#989895`: secondary type
- `#b8f13f`: WalletBrief accent and wristband
- `#ff9d86`: warning only

## Type

- Inter or Arial for plain, high-contrast copy
- SF Mono or Menlo for addresses and proof labels

## Motion

- Film cuts stay direct and short.
- Product proof enters with a fast ease-out and a slow two-percent push.
- Captions use one fixed lower-third position.
- One focal point per beat.
- The end card holds long enough to read.

## Do not use

- Screenshot carousels
- Generic purple tech gradients
- Floating glass cards
- Large blocks of copy
- Glitch effects or fake chain data
